Take Your Sweet Time...

Feb 19, 2020

The most important lesson I learned in this year.

认识到自己的普通, 但不要失去自信… 这之中的秘密就是努力做好小事的能力, 它会带给一个人高贵的气质…

请选择和时间做朋友… THINK BIG, THINK LONG…

Savor your life, don’t worry. The common sense will become more and more clear… Believe me, it’s not easy.

“You don’t have a lot of envy.”
“You don’t have a lot of resentment.”
“You don’t overspend your income.”
“You stay cheerful in spite of your troubles.”
“You deal with reliable people.”
“You do what you’re supposed to do.”
And all these simple rules work so well to make your life better. And they’re so trite.

from Charlie Munger

……

“Do the thing you fear and the death of fear is certain.”

from Ralph Waldo Emerson

……

Nothing in this world can take the place of persistence.
Talent will not; nothing is more common than unsuccessful men with talent.
Genius will not; unrewarded genius is almost a proverb.
Education will not; the world is full of educated derelicts.
Persistence and determination alone are omnipotent.

from Calvin Coolidge

……

Your security rests in knowing how to do a variety of things, and being able to do them better than others.

from Lloyd Blankfein

In the past, I know that we should make friends with excellent people, but I still underestimate the influence, actually I think your social connections build you, means you always implicitly mimic your friend’s behavior. Let’s pursue good things, and the only one principle… “Learn by doing things”…
