Good Habits

Jul 20, 2020

Cultivate myself, towards a better and more rational person… It’s a life-long persuit!

I like this quote very much, I write this article because now I know “the secret” lies behind “the happy life”… When you want something, you needs put a lot of hard work, remember there is no free lunch in the world. I admitted that, almost all of my previous unhappiness came from my bad personality, but this is very difficult to change at that time, because the surrounding is not very good, lots of people in my family have no opportunity to receive decent education. But now now I have the opportunity to learn from great people, including their intellectual ability, but even more important, is that learn how to confront troubles(means that we need get the work done, yet also keep a growth mindset, don’t blame others).

Rewarding meself, even if there are only small changes, the subconscious behaviors are much much more powerful than I think. But we have no choice about the fixed things, what we only can do is change our perspective, think about how can I improve myself, from my perspective, the best way is that we can immitate the excellent individuals. Gradually, you will finish “fake it until you make it” process, then the miracle happens.

I can clearly feel that inside me there are many not good nerual links inside me, maybe due to inappropriate education from parents, but it’s my duty to change myself, don’t blame others. I am grateful I have the opportunity to receive relatively good education from university, this teaches me what’s right and what’s wrong… And more importantly, I know how to change myself through conscious trainning.

I read many excellent articles, the core of them is taking responsibility of your life, it’s your duty to make it better than before. If you are unsatisfied with your current situation, no one will come, it’s all about your internal desire to become better.
