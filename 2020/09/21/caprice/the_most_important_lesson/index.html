The Most Important Lesson

Sep 21, 2020

Based on limited life experience, I know the biggest challenge is to know how to get along with internal emotions peacefully and elegantly.

This is the very meaning of “be yourself” or “be all you can be”.

If you ask me, what’s the most important thing… that working abroad teaches me? I would say, it’s this. Obviously, “easier said than done”… especially from not very successful family.

But I can guarantee it’s doomed to failure, if I lack of this ability. When somone really has knew what’s the true meaning of “psychological maturity” is, he will understand what I am talking about.
